---

## Executive Summary

v1.12 establishes the pinned NIP-5D contract as the repo-local authority and brings the playground shell, shared runtime/shim surface, and all 13 playground napplets into conformance with that contract.

The milestone repaired stale AUTH/REGISTER/NIP-01 protocol-identity drift, made shell capability state authoritative for hosted `window.napplet.shell.supports()`, surfaced manifest `requires` through gateway metadata, rejected unsupported required NUBs before iframe navigation, and added source/static/E2E guards for the drift classes identified in `.planning/NIP-5D-DELTA-AUDIT.md`.

All remaining deviations are documented as deliberate Kehto NUB extensions or demo/test-only raw-envelope exceptions. Full verification passed.
